Discord Music Bot

This Discord Music Bot allows users to play music in voice channels directly from various sources, including YouTube and Spotify. It features commands for playing, pausing, skipping, and stopping music, along with a queue system for managing multiple tracks. The bot is designed to enhance the listening experience in Discord servers, making it easy for users to enjoy music together in real time.
